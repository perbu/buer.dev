Weenect

Weenect makes GPS trackers for pets. I use them to keep tabs on my cats. These are tools I've built around their platform.

cat2k

A dashboard for Weenect GPS trackers and Sureflap cat flaps. Shows real-time location and tracks your cat's outdoor habits over time, rendered as heatmaps so you can see where they actually spend their time.

github.com/perbu/cat2k

weenect-go

A Go client library for the Weenect API. Handles authentication, device listing, and position retrieval. Used by cat2k under the hood.

github.com/perbu/weenect-go
